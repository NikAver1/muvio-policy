1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional and personal fitness tracking experience, the App utilizes various device services and handles the following data types:

A. Account and Profile Information

  • Authentication Data: Email address and credentials (managed securely via Google Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In) to establish and maintain your user account.
  • Profile Information: Name, age, gender, weight, height, and general fitness goals entered voluntarily to calculate exercise metrics.

B. Health, Activity, and Sensor Data

  • Pedometer & Step Counts: Direct device step sensor data to record your daily step counts and calculate active calorie burn.
  • Workout Logs: Customized exercises, durations, weights, and repetitions logged within the application.

C. Device and Permission-Based Data

  • Location Data (Geolocator): With your explicit consent, we access your device's location to track your real-time running, walking, or cycling routes on active maps. Location tracking runs exclusively during active workout sessions and is not tracked in the background unless explicitly authorized.
  • Camera Access: Utilized for scanning QR codes or barcodes (via Mobile Scanner) or taking progress/profile pictures.
  • Audio & Microphone (Record API): Utilized if you engage in voice controls or audio note captures within App workflows.

D. Third-Party Integrations & AI Systems

  • Google Generative AI (Gemini API): The App contains interactive AI tools that process your logged fitness metrics securely via Google Generative AI to provide customized health recommendations. No personally identifying information is sold to third parties.

2. Data Retention Policy

In accordance with Google Play developer guidelines, we maintain a clear data retention structure:

Retention Period: Your user profile, workout histories, and files are stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore and Storage as long as your user account remains active. If your account is completely inactive for a continuous period of twenty-four (24) months, we reserve the right to securely purge and permanently delete all your user data from our databases.

3. Data Deletion Policy (Your Right to Erasure)

We respect your privacy and provide immediate, unhindered control over your personal data.

How to Delete Your Data:
1. In-App Deletion: You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated physical logs instantly by navigating to the App's **Settings** screen and clicking the **"Delete Account"** button.
2. Manual Deletion Request: If you cannot access the App, you may email us directly at avernik1804@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will permanently delete your account and all associated cloud-hosted data within seven (7) business days of receiving your request.

4. Data Protection and Security

We employ robust industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. All cloud data transmissions utilize Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption, and database structures are hosted on secure, restricted Google Firebase server frameworks. However, no database transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.
